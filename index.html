Welcome to VIDA

VIDA is a by-invitation-only travel event crafted for high-net-worth travel connoisseurs. Hosted at the iconic Le Negresco, it connects the UK’s most elite travel curators with Europe’s most distinguished hotels and resorts. Set against the elegance of the French Riviera, VIDA is three days of powerful connections, meaningful dialogue, and world-class hospitality.

Who Should Attend?

Travel Advisors – Meet Europe’s finest hoteliers, discover the latest in luxury, and elevate your clients’ experiences.

Hotel Executives – Gain direct access to top-tier UK agencies and build lasting, productive relationships with influential travel designers.

  • Panel discussions and curated roundtables
